The Problem

A Language Barrier
Killing Potential

Millions of students across India study in English-medium schools despite their mother tongue being Hindi, Bengali, Tamil, Telugu, or one of hundreds of other languages.

When a student doesn't understand a concept, they have nowhere to go. Private tutors are expensive. Online platforms are English-centric. Parents may not have the education to help.

VidyaBot solves this by being a patient, always-available tutor that speaks the student's own language — completely free.
